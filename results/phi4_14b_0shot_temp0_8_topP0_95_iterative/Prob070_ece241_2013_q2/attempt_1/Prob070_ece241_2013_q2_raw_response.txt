- **15**: `a=1`, `b=1`, `c=1`, `d=1` → `a & b & c & d`

\[ \text{out\_sop} = (\sim a \land \sim b \land c \land \sim d) \lor (\sim a \land b \land c \land d) \lor (a \land b \land c \land d) \]
